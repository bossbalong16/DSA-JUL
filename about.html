About PUPShop

PUPShop is the official merchandise store of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. We aim to provide students, alumni, and supporters with high-quality apparel, accessories, and other merchandise that showcases the pride and spirit of PUP.

Our store offers a wide range of T-shirts, jerseys, and laces, all designed with the PUP logo and colors. We are committed to excellent customer service, fast delivery, and ensuring that every purchase is a satisfying experience for our customers.

Join our community of proud PUPians by wearing our merchandise and showing off your school pride wherever you go!
